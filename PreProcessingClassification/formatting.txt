$F_{mean}$ : Mean of the data points
$F_{min}$ : Minimum of the data points
$F_{max}$ : Maximum of the data points
$F_{sum}$ : Sum of the data points
$F_{prod}$ : Product of the data points
$F_{std}$ : Standard deviation of the data points
$F_{mc}$ : Mean crossing of the data points
$F_{zc}$ : Zero crossing of the data points
$F_{iqr}$ : Interquartile range of the data points
$F_{skew}$ : Skewness of the data points
$F_{25}$ : 25th percentile of the data points
$F_{75}$ : 75th percentile of the data points
$F_{kr}$ : Kurtosis of the data points
$F_{seg}$ : Segmentation of the data points
$F_{sep}$ : Separation of the data points
